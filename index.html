LoopRun – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

LoopRun ("we", "our", "us") is a running route generator app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data We Collect

LoopRun collects your GPS location solely to generate running routes near your current position. Location data is never stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use OpenRouteService API to generate routes. Your coordinates are sent to their servers to calculate routes. Please refer to their privacy policy at openrouteservice.org.

We use OpenStreetMap for map tiles. Please refer to their privacy policy at osmfoundation.org.

4. Data Storage

LoopRun does not store any personal data on external servers. Route history (if any) is stored locally on your device only.

5. Permissions

  • Location (Fine & Coarse): Required to generate routes from your current position.
  • Internet: Required to load map tiles and generate routes via API.

6. Children's Privacy

LoopRun is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
